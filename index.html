HCAA Mandatory Procedures ▲
Required by the Hellenic Civil Aviation Authority (HCAA) for all UAS operations within the Karpathos ATZ / CTR.
  • CALL Initial coordination call: Contact the tower in advance to announce the planned operation and confirm airspace availability for the requested sectors.
  • CALL Pre-flight clearance call: Call the tower immediately before commencing flight to receive final sector clearance and check for active traffic.
  • STBY Mobile standby: Keep your mobile phone on standby throughout the entire operation — the tower may need to reach you at any moment.
  • LAND Immediate safe landing: Land immediately upon detecting or being informed of any manned aircraft or helicopter approaching the area.
  • CALL Post-flight notification: Call the tower immediately after landing to confirm airspace is clear and the operation is complete.
